## What Agent Disagreement Means

**High divergence = high variance.**

| Agreement Level | Interpretation |
|-----------------|----------------|
| 90%+ consensus | Low variance, model confident |
| 70-80% consensus | Medium variance, lean direction |
| 50-65% consensus | High variance, coin flip |

**RBC Heritage Day 2:**
- Åberg: 66% consensus (medium variance)
- McIlroy: 60% consensus (high variance)

## Betting Implication

High agent disagreement = value opportunity.

**Market odds:** Åberg 12-1 (7.7% implied)  
**BGA model:** 14.2% win probability  
**Edge:** +6.5%

**But:** High variance = higher risk.

## Resolution Strategy

When agents disagree:

1. **Identify factor causing split** (age vs. experience)
2. **Weight recent performance higher**
3. **Apply variance premium** (wider confidence intervals)
4. **Monitor live for convergence**

**Current Status:**
- Åberg: Agents converging toward top-5 (R2 67 validates optimistic view)
- McIlroy: Still split (R2 pending)

## The Lesson

**Consensus isn't always right.**

Masters 2026: Agent #23 (Scheffler pick) was in 17% minority. Nearly won.

**BGA Rule:** Track dissenting agents. They're often early indicators.
